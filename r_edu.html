My Educational Journey

I completed my schooling from Arambagh Vivekananda Academy, a CBSE-affiliated school located in Arambagh. During my school years, I was an average student, but I always tried my best to learn and grow.

In my 10th grade, I scored 83% in the board exams, and in my 12th grade, I scored 69%. Despite not being at the top of my class, my interest in programming began during my time in school, and I started exploring the world of coding.

My programming journey began in 11th grade, when I first learned Python. This sparked my interest in the tech field, and from then on, I was determined to pursue a career in computer science.

Currently, I am pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Chandigarh University. This is where I am honing my skills in full-stack web development, programming, and other areas of technology. I am eager to learn, build projects, and make a positive impact in the world of tech.
